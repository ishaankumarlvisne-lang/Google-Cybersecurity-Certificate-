## what is cybersecurity 
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems,networks and data from digital attacks
## Malware and Virus
### Computer Virus
Malicious code written to interfere with computer operations and cause damage
### Malware
Software designed to harm devices or networks
## Historical Attacks
### Brain Virus ( 1986)
 First known PC virus
 Spread through floppy disk
 did not destroy data but slowed system performance
 ### Morris Virus 
 Designed to measure the internet size 
 Replicated itself repeatedly
 Caused systems to crash due to overload
 ### Why study Attacks 
 Helps anticipate evolving threats
 Develop better defenses
 Improve Incident response

 ## CIA Triad
 **Confidentiality** - Data should be accessed only by authorized users
 **Integrity** - Data should remain accurate and unaltered
 **Availability** - Data should be accessed when needed
